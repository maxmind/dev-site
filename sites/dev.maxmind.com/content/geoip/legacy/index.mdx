<Alert type="warning">
We are retiring our GeoIP Legacy **database** formats at the end of May 2022.
Please see our [blog post](https://blog.maxmind.com/2020/06/01/retirement-of-geoip-legacy-downloadable-databases-in-may-2022/)
for more information.

We are planning to make a number of data changes to the minFraud Legacy
Standard and Premium, and GeoIP Legacy City and Insights (formerly Omni) web
services at the end of May 2022. Please see our [blog post](https://blog.maxmind.com/2020/06/15/data-changes-to-geoip-legacy-and-minfraud-legacy-web-services-in-may-2022/)
for more information, and how to prepare.
</Alert>

<Alert>

</Alert>
